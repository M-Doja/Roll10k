Roll10k

    Rules: Make it to 10,000 points to win

      * Roll a 1 = 100pts

      * Roll a 5 = 50pts

      * Roll three 1's = 1000pts

      * Roll 3 of a kind = The rolled number multiplied by 100pts
      The exception is 1's

      * Roll 4 of a kind = The rolled number multiplied by 100pts + 100pts

      * Roll 5 of a kind = If fives 1's are rolled you get 10,000 pts. You Win!
      Otherwise you get the rolled number multiplied by 100pts + 200pts

      * Click number to save points and to continue rolling remaining numbers.
      However if you roll and get no points you lose all points from your current turn
      and your turn is over.
